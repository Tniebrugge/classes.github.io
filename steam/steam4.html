The fourth level of Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics (S.T.E.A.M.) is more complex and begins to allow students to begin to choose their own path. There is only one project that is is preselected for students to complete for the semester. Beyond the first project, students can choose from a myriad of options to continue their studies in S.T.E.A.M.

S.T.E.A.M. 4 Projects:

  • T-Shirt Launcher

T-Shirt Launcher


Students will utilize knowledge and skills to build a t-shirt launcher out of scraps of wood. The t-shirt launcher should be able to launch t-shirts into the stands from the middle of the football field. General design specifications will be given for students to build their t-shirt launcher, but the decoration and minor details will remain for the student to determine.
